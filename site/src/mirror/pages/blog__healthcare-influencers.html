Top 17 Healthcare Influencers in UK to Follow -True Homecare

Saima Adil Zafar

25 October, 2024

Nowadays, as Instagram, Facebook, and other social media platforms have become an integral part of our lives and the healthcare community, they have given healthcare influencers the means to connect with their target audience while spreading and shaping public healthcare awareness.  These healthcare influencers, whether they be doctors, nutritionists, fitness coaches, or mental health advocates, share their knowledge on social media in the form of posts, blogs, and podcasts, which are easily accessible. These healthcare influencers are not merely branding themselves but inspiring individuals to take charge of their health. 

We have compiled a list of top healthcare professionals to follow on social media. Following them would keep you updated on the latest health trends, expert advice, and critical wellness tips. 

Here’s a rundown of the top healthcare influencers in the UK you need to follow.

17 Top UK Healthcare Influencers

Dr Rangan Chatterjee

Dr. Rangan Chatterjee is one of the most prominent healthcare influencers in the UK. He’s a GP, author, and host of the popular podcast “Feel Better, Live More.” Dr. Chatterjee’s mission is to help people feel good about themselves by helping them restore their optimal health. He advocates for a holistic approach to health and focuses on lifestyle medicine that includes diet, sleep, exercise, and mental wellbeing. 

Over the course the course of his career, he has helped his patients:

  • Reverse type 2 diabetes
  • Eliminate irritable bowel syndrome
  • Lower blood pressure without drugs
  • Reduce menopausal symptoms naturally
  • Sleep better and regain their energy
  • Regain control of their autoimmune conditions
  • Restore harmony to their circadian rhythms
  • Add life to their years, as well as years to their life

Specialty/Area of Focus: 

Lifestyle medicine, mental health, and preventive care.

Social Media Presence: 

Website: https://drchatterjee.com/ 

Instagram: @drchatterjee 

Facebook: Dr Chatterjee

Twitter: @drchatterjeeuk

Youtube: @DrChatterjeeRangan

Impact and Contributions: 

Dr. Chatterjee has changed how people look at preventive health through his books and media appearances. His holistic approach to health has encouraged thousands of people to adopt a healthier lifestyle. 

He is also the star of the BBC One series – Doctor in the House – where he visits different families in their homes and investigates every aspect of their lives. By the time he leaves, the families hope to lead a happier and healthier life. 

Dr. Rupy Aujla

Dr. Rupy Aujla, an NHS doctor and author, is the founder of “The Doctor’s Kitchen,” which is a platform that shows how food and slight changes in your eating habits can affect your health. He aims to inspire everyone to cook delicious, healthy meals to improve their health. Dr. Aujla’s recipes are based on medical science, which makes him one of the leading UK health influencers in nutrition and medicine.

Specialty/Area of Focus: 

Nutrition, lifestyle medicine, culinary medicine.

Social Media Presence:

Website: https://www.thedoctorskitchen.com/ 

Instagram: @doctors_kitchen

Facebook: The Doctor’s Kitchen

Twitter: @doctors_kitchen

YouTube: @doctors_kitchen

Impact or Contributions: 

He believes in regaining health and healing yourself through food. His website offers 800 research-backed recipes and personalized food for unique health needs. 

Dr Hazel Wallace

Dr. Hazel Wallace is a medical doctor, registered associate nutritionist, personal trainer, and founder of “The Food Medic,” which is a platform that is all about health, fitness, and nutrition. She believes nutrition and exercise have the power to prevent and manage chronic illnesses. She works as a top healthcare influencer in the UK and offers a balanced approach to health that combines modern medicine with wellness practices.

Specialty/Area of Focus: 

Nutrition, fitness, lifestyle medicine.

Social Media Presence: 

Website: https://www.thefoodmedic.co.uk/ 

Instagram: @thefoodmedic

Facebook: The Food Medic

Twitter: @Thefoodmedic

Impact or Contributions: 

Dr. Hazel Wallace holds a The Food Medic podcast, in which she invites and interviews leading experts across the field of health and nutrition. Her book, The Female Factor, provides expert tips on how women can protect and improve their health. She also talks of the role of diet and exercise in maintaining health and wellbeing. 

Rhiannon Lambert

Rhiannon Lambert is one of the leading nutritionists and influencers in healthcare in the UK. She is the founder of a renowned clinic, Rhitrition, which specializes in weight management, sports nutrition, eating disorders, and pre and post-natal nutrition. They have a highly qualified team of nutritionists, dietitians, and psychologists who work with individuals to transform their lives. 

Specialty/Area of Focus: 

Weight management, sports nutrition, eating disorders, and  pre and post-natal nutrition

Social Media Presence: 

Website: https://www.rhitrition.com/ 

Instagram: @rhitrition

Facebook: Rhitrition

Twitter: @Rhitrition

Youtube: @Rhitrition

Impact or Contributions: 

Rhitrition offers clear answers that help you make informed decisions for yourself about what, when, and how to eat responsibly. 

Madeleine Shaw

Madeleine Shaw has been working as a healthcare industry expert in the UK for ten years. She started her journey as a nutritionist and home cook and later trained to be a yoga and meditation teacher. She believes in thinking holistically about health, taking into account what you are eating and how you are moving. When you start thinking holistically, you actually start to see the greatest results.

Specialty/Area of Focus: 

Nutritionist & home cook, Yoga and meditation teacher

Social Media Presence: 

Website: https://www.madeleineshaw.com/ 

Instagram: @madeleine_shaw_

Impact or Contributions:

Madeleine Shaw created a wellness hub – The Glow Space – where she endeavors to teach people how to incorporate principles of a healthy and happy life into their busy & varied lives.

Sarah’s Day

Sarah Day is a health and fitness influencer who shares workout routines, healthy recipes, and insights into healthy living. She promotes functional fitness and body positivity while sharing her own personal health journey with her followers.

Specialty/Area of Focus: 

Functional fitness, holistic health, natural living

Social Media Presence: 

Website: https://sarahsday.com/ 

Instagram: @sarahs_day

Youtube: @SarahsDay

Impact or Contributions:

Sarah’s approach to health and wellness is relatable and has empowered women to embrace their bodies and focus on their mental and physical wellbeing. 

Dr Louise Newson

Dr. Louise Newson is a famous menopause specialist in the UK. She runs the Newson Health Menopause and Wellbeing Centre and provides evidence-based care for women. Dr. Newson also founded the Balance app. This app is designed to help women manage and track their menopause symptoms. She is one of the leading voices behind better education around menopause and hormone replacement therapy (HRT).

Specialty/Area of Focus: 

Menopause, perimenopause, HRT

Social Media Presence: 

Website: https://www.drlouisenewson.co.uk/ 

Instagram: @menopause_doctor

LinkedIn: Louise Newson

Twitter: @drlouisenewson

Youtube: @menopause_doctor

Impact or Contributions:

She has worked tirelessly to raise awareness regarding menopause and has worked for a better healthcare system for women who are going through menopause.

Zanna Van Dijk is a fitness trainer. She released her first book, strong, in 2016, which taught women about lifting weights and the role of exercise in staying healthy. She is also the funder of a sustainable, ethical, and beautiful swimwear and essentials brand – Stay Wild. Zanna’s fitness content is accessible to all, and she frequently stands for mental health alongside physical fitness.

Specialty/Area of Focus:

Fitness, sustainable living, mental health 

Social Media Presence:

Facebook: Zanna van Dijk

Functional fitness, body positivity, motherhood
