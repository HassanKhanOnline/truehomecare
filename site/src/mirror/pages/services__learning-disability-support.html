Our Learning Disability Support Services

We at True Homecare offer a wide range of support services designed to meet your specific needs. Here is what we have to offer.

Community Access

We help individuals participate in community activities and access essential resources, such as providing support in attending social events, educational programs, and employment opportunities. We also take our clients out for movies, hospital visits, and for walks in the park.

Transportation Support

A significant barrier for individuals with learning disabilities can be transportation. Hence, we at True Homecare provide transportation support so our clients can participate in educational, social, and employment activities without any hindrance.

Skill Development

Our goal is to empower individuals with the skills they need to lead a fulfilling life. Hence, we offer support in developing essential skills for independent living, education, and employment.

Emotional Support

We understand that emotional wellbeing is crucial for overall health. Our team provides emotional support to our clients and their families, ensuring they feel understood, valued, and cared for throughout their journey with us.

24-hour Support

True Homecare offers 24-hour support to ensure that our clients receive the care they need at any time of the day or night. Whether it’s waking nights or sleeping nights, we are here to provide continuous and reliable care.

Ethical Framework by Stockport Council

We work on the Ethical Framework by Stockport Council, ensuring that all our services adhere to the highest standards of ethical care. This framework guides our practices, ensuring that we deliver compassionate and respectful care to all our clients.
