How do I arrange Private Companionship Services?

Our local Cheshire and Greater Manchester care team will guide you seamlessly through the process of arranging a safeguarding-compliant companionship package tailored perfectly to your loved one's personality and social needs.

1. Contact our Companionship Experts

Get in touch with our Stockport and Wilmslow-based team using our secure online enquiry form to discuss your family’s specific options. Our dedicated support team is available 24/7 and aims to respond to all adult social care enquiries immediately to provide instant peace of mind.

2. Free In-Home Assessment within 24 hours

Our experienced local Care Managers will discuss your requirements, learn more about your loved one’s hobbies and daily routine, and carry out a free assessment in their own home. This helps us understand the exact type of emotional support and befriending required to effectively combat loneliness.

3. Your bespoke Companionship package

Our team will create a fully person-centred care package based on your exact needs and preferences. We never do random placements; all of our CQC-rated staff are carefully matched based on the individual personality traits, shared interests, and specific cognitive requirements of your loved one.

Combatting Loneliness with Expert Emotional Support and CQC-Rated Care

Social isolation can significantly impact a senior’s mental and physical health, often becoming a primary challenge for concerned families. True Homecare addresses this by providing consistent, meaningful human connection that brings immediate emotional relief to both clients and relatives. Our professional companions offer emotional stability and mental stimulation to reduce anxiety, ensuring your loved one feels valued while meeting the highest standards of our Care Quality Commission (CQC) rated ‘Good’ adult social care.

Committed to Comfort and Quality

Your comfort is our priority. From the moment you reach out, our dedicated team ensures that your care plan is not only effective but also reflects the high standards of quality you deserve. We deliver care that aligns with your lifestyle, allowing you to thrive at home.

Where Expertise Meets Compassion

Each member of our team brings a wealth of experience, training, and a genuine passion for providing exceptional care. We’re here to help you live comfortably and independently, with the support you need, when you need it.

Care as Unique as You Are

Every person is different, and so are their care needs. That’s why we offer flexible, bespoke care solutions that cater to your specific requirements. Whether it’s companionship, specialist care, or assistance with daily tasks, we tailor our services to support your health, happiness, and wellbeing at home.

Our Comprehensive Companionship Support Features

Social Engagement & Befriending

Our companions provide meaningful conversation and engage in shared hobbies, ensuring your loved one remains socially active and emotionally connected through genuine, trusted human interaction and dedicated befriending.

Community Access & Outings

We provide safe accompaniment to medical appointments, social clubs, and local events, helping seniors maintain their community ties and navigate the outside world confidently with a trained care professional.

Mental & Cognitive Stimulation

To support cognitive health, our staff engage clients in puzzles, reading, and memory games, providing the mental exercise necessary to keep the mind sharp and prevent cognitive decline effectively.

Light Household Help & Errands

We assist with light housekeeping and meal preparation, reducing the stress of daily chores so your loved one can focus on enjoying their time and improving their overall wellbeing.

Digital Connectivity & Tech Support

Our team helps seniors stay connected by assisting with video calls to family and managing digital paperwork, bridging the gap between traditional living and modern technology with patient guidance.

Routine Wellness Checks & Safety

We provide consistent monitoring of emotional states and physical safety, offering peace of mind by identifying changes in wellbeing and ensuring a secure, hazard-free home environment every single day.

The True Homecare Standard: Authority & Core Values

The Matching Process

We don’t believe in random assignments. Our team conducts a detailed personality assessment to pair seniors with carers who share similar interests, hobbies, and values, ensuring a natural friendship develops from the first visit.

Step-by-Step Care Planning

We build a bespoke framework for your loved one’s daily life:

  • Step 1: Initial In-Home Assessment to gauge mobility and social needs.
  • Step 2: Matching with a fully-vetted local Care Professional.
  • Step 3: Creation of a personalized social calendar mapping out activities and visits.
  • Step 4: Ongoing reviews to adapt the care plan as needs evolve.

Skilled and Certified Caregivers

Quality is maintained through rigorous staff vetting. Every companion undergoes comprehensive background checks, safeguarding protocols, and specialized training to ensure they meet our professional standards for safety, ethics, and elderly care aligned with UK healthcare regulations.

Mental Health Focus

Consistent companionship is a vital tool in maintaining cognitive health. By providing regular social interaction and mental stimulation, we help reduce the risk of cognitive decline and protect against the severe health effects of chronic loneliness.

Companionship Care vs. Residential Facilities

Understanding your options is critical. While care homes offer structured environments, our private companionship services empower seniors to remain happily and safely in their own beloved homes.
